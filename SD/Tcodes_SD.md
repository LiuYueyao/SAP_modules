# SD T-codes

## sales order
* VA01/VA02/VA03
  * create/change/display sales documents

## invoice
* MIRO
  * enter incoming invoice
* MIR4/MIR5
  * display invoice documents

## delivery
* VL01N/VL02N/VL03N
  * create/change/display outbound delivery

## billing
* VF01/VF02/VF03
  * create/change/display billing document
* VF11
  * cancel billing document
